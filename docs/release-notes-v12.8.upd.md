Percona Distribution for PostgreSQL is a collection of tools to assist you in managing PostgreSQL. Percona Distribution for PostgreSQL
installs PostgreSQL and complements it by a selection of extensions that
enable solving essential practical tasks efficiently.

This update of Percona Distribution for PostgreSQL includes the new version of [pg_stat_monitor](https://github.com/percona/pg_stat_monitor) 1.0.0-RC - the statistics collection tool for PostgreSQL.

We welcome your feedback on your experience with `pg_stat_monitor` in the [public JIRA project](https://jira.percona.com/projects/DISTPG).
